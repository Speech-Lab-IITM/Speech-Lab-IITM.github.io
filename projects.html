April-2021: Project positions immediately available in Automatic Speech Recognition!!!

You will build state-of-the-art ASR systems using deep learning ideas such as sequence-to-sequence modelling
based on CTC, Transformers, BERT & GPT as well as conventional DNN, CNN and TDNN.
Applicants who have completed B.E./B.Tech./M.Tech are encouraged to apply via e-mail to
speechiitm@ee.iitm.ac.in

Description :

The Speech Processing Lab headed by Prof. S. Umesh in the Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Madras
is seeking applications for the post of Project Associate. They will be working on Natural Language
Translation Mission (NLTM) of the Govt. of India. The objective is to develop Indian Language technology
systems and products and deploy them in the field with the help of start-ups. Prof. Umesh is leading the
Automatic Speech Recognition part ofthe project. The candidate’s day to day role would involve building
state of the art ASR Systems in Indian languages using deep learning ideas including sequence-to-sequence
modelling based on CTC, transformers,wav2vec, BERT and GPT as well as conventional DNN, CNN and TDNN.

Required Skills : Python, Deep Learning and PyTorch
Duration : 1 year
No of Positions : 3
Who Can Apply :
  • Applicants who have B.E./B.Tech or higher degree in Computer Science or Electrical Engineering.
  • Have prior knowledge and experience with Deep Learning related problems.
  • Experience with pytorch.
  • Available for a time period of one year starting from March 2021.
Other Preferred Skills :
  • Having in-depth knowledge of fields such as Speech Processing, NLP (Natural Language Processing) or Vision
  • Should be comfortable with working remotely.
  • Comfortable in using Linux, git.
Salary : Commensurate with experience under IITM rules
Contact : Please send your resume to: speechiitm@ee.iitm.ac.in
Last Date 1st may 2021 (rolling advertisement until positions are filled)
